Covid Regulation

Due to the COVID-19, We will follow Taiwan government policy. WFU will offer airport pick-up service for new international students from Taoyuan International Airport. Once you arrive the airport, our staff will assist you to check in at “overseas students reception counter” and accompany you to take the quarantine taxi to the quarantine dorm or hotel.

The further information is as following: Please contact NaWFUa’s staffs to make sure the visa applying, accommodation arranging are on progress before buying the flight ticket. We will assist and reply the suitable arrival date to you as soon as possible. After buying the ticket, make sure to provide your flight information to the staff in order to let us applying entry permit from the Ministry of Education. You are allowed to enter Taiwan once the entry permit is issued.


Eligibility Check

Overseas students applying to NTU are divided into International Students, Overseas Chinese Students, Hong Kong and Macao Students, and Mainland Chinese Students. The application process, tuition fees, and available degrees may differ depending on students' identities. Before you start your application to WFU, it is important to check your eligibility and decide the application methods you would like to use to submit your application.


Academic Requirements

International applicants with a high school diploma are eligible to apply for undergraduate programs.

International applicants with a bachelor's degree are eligible to apply for master's degree programs.


Application dates

The application period for Fall semester is starting from now to 20 Aug 2021.

The application period for Spring semester is starting from now to 20 Jan 2022.
