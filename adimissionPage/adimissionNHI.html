Background and Regulations

According to the National Health Insurance (NHI) Act, any foreigner who has stayed in Taiwan for over six months is obligated to underwrite in the NHI system. How do I, a new student, know if I am qualified to join NHI?
Counting from the issue date of your ARC, you are qualified to join NHI if you have stayed in Taiwan for 6 consecutive months. Within this 6 month period, you are only allowed to leave Taiwan once for no longer than 29 days. You are qualified to join NHI if you are certain that you have not violated this regulation.



Costs and Payments

The cost of NHI is included in the tuition fee for each semester. After students have paid the tuition and completed the underwriting procedure at Taipei Tech, the Bureau of National Health Insurance will issue payment forms to the university to pay the insurance fee on behalf of the students. The cost is NT$ 749 each month, which means a total of NT$4,494 (NT$749x6) for each of the two semesters in a year. The amount of NT$4,494 will be paid for the first semester (six months starting from September to February) and subsequently, the same amount for the second semester (six months starting from March to August).

Guidelines for Insuring at Taipei Tech

Scenario One
Newly arrived students who have stayed in Taiwan for over 6 months :
The staff of the Office of Student Affairs (OSA) will email you for filling in the NHI IC cards application form. The application forms will be submitted to the Bureau of National Health Insurance in the middle and the end of each month by the university. The procedure will take about two weeks, and the IC card will be sent directly to the mailing address that you provided on your application form.

Scenario Two
Newly arrived students who have NOT stayed in Taiwan for over 6 months :
Once you have stayed in Taiwan for over 6 months, please follow the procedure outlined in Scenario 1 to underwrite in the NHI system.

Scenario Three
Students who have underwritten NHI under other associations or schools but would like to transfer their NHI accounts to Taipei Tech :
Please check if there is an “insurance fee” item on your semester tuition receipt.
Yes : Please refer to your previous agency (language school, company, or municipal administration, etc.) for an insurance policy closure document. Bring the closure document and tuition receipt of the current semester to the Overseas Student Advising Section to transfer your NHI to Taipei Tech.
No : Please refer to your previous agency (language school, company, or municipal administration, etc.) for an insurance policy closure document. Then bring the document to the Overseas Student Advising Section of the Office of Student Affairs to get the payment form and pay at the Cashier of the Office of General Affairs. After payment, please return to the Overseas Student Advising Section again with the payment receipt to transfer your NHI to Taipei Tech.


Renewal of the NHI IC Card

Please apply at the Bureau of National Health Insurance. The renewal cost is $NT 200.
