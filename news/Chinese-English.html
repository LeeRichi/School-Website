"Chinese-English Translation and Cultural Courses" from NTU

1. The International Affairs Office of our school will hold an online English course of "Chinese-English Translation and Cultural Course" from July 5th to July 24th, 110. The content covers:
(1) Translation Theory
(2) Chinese-English translation
(3) Chinese-English translation
(4) Translation of Taiwanese Literature
2. Those who apply for and complete this course and relevant requirements will receive 3 credits of the school’s international short-term courses and a certificate of study.
3. The course registration period is from now until May 16, for course introduction and online registration, please go to our school website https://oiasystem.ntu.edu.tw/summer/course/index.detail/season/2/course_sn/218/ intro/1875 or scan the QR Code shown in the attachment.
