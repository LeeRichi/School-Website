Feng Chia University English summer camp updates

一、為促進外籍學生了解我國文化與增進學習華語文興趣，並 展現對臺灣的熱愛及分享多元觀點，特舉辦本項比賽。
二、旨揭活動訊息如下：
(一)報名日期：110年9月1日至10月8日止。
(二)比賽日期：110年11月16日(星期二)上午9時開始。
(三)比賽地點：國立國父紀念館演講廳。
三、本活動僅限通訊報名。報名注意事項請詳閱比賽簡章，逾 期或資料不全者，不另通知補件，視同報名無效。
四、配合中央流行疫情指揮中心規定，屆時若疫情發展嚴峻， 本活動將暫緩辦理，相關訊息請密切注意本館官方網站 「最新消息」公告，不另行通知。


1. To promote foreign students to understand Chinese culture and increase their interest in learning Chinese, and Demonstrating love for Taiwan and sharing diverse views, this competition is specially held.
2. The announcement of the event information is as follows:
(1) Registration date: September 1st to October 8th, 110.
(2) Competition date: November 16, 110 (Tuesday) at 9 o'clock in the morning.
(3) The venue of the competition: the lecture hall of the National Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall.
3. This activity is only for correspondence registration. Please read the competition brochure carefully for the matters needing attention in registration. If the deadline or information is incomplete, no additional notice will be given, and the registration will be deemed invalid.
4. In accordance with the regulations of the Central Epidemic Command Center, if the epidemic develops severely by then, This activity will be postponed, please pay close attention to the official website of the museum for relevant information "Latest News" announcement.
