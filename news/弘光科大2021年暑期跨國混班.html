Hungkuang University summer camp

一、本校辦理之暑期跨國混班內容概述如下：
(一)活動時間分兩梯次：
１、2021年8月2日至8月7日。
２、2021年8月8日至8月14日。
(二)申請資格：台灣各大專校院之在籍境外生 (母語非中文者)。
(三)活動內容：
１、手作課程。
２、文化之旅。
３、跨文化溝通工作坊。
(四)活動費用：免費(不含機票費、個人消費)。
二、此活動有名額限制，報名截止日：2021年6月30日，報名連結：https://forms.gle/ehubKQ9MCvo8A3gw8。
三、歡迎各校在籍境外生踴躍報名參加，有興趣者可洽詢本校國際暨兩岸事務處李昭妮代理組長(peyton5400@hk.edu.tw/ 04-26318652#2232)。
四、疫情期間，本校一切遵照教育部及中央流行疫情指揮中心之防疫規定。


1. The summer cross-border mixed classes handled by our school are summarized as follows:
(1) The activity time is divided into two levels:
1.August 2 to August 7, 2021.
2.August 8 to August 14, 2021.
(2) Qualifications for application: foreign students (non-native Chinese) who are currently studying in Taiwan's colleges and universities.
(3) Activity content:
1. Hand-made courses.
2. Cultural journey.
3. Cross-cultural communication workshop.
(4) Activity fee: Free (excluding air ticket fee and personal consumption).
2. There are quotas for this activity. Registration deadline: June 30, 2021. Registration link: https://forms.gle/ehubKQ9MCvo8A3gw8.
3. Overseas students from all schools are welcome to register actively. Those who are interested can contact Li Zhaoni, the acting head of the International and Cross-Strait Affairs Office of the school (peyton5400@hk.edu.tw/ 04-26318652#2232).
4. During the pandemic, the school shall comply with the pandemic prevention regulations of the Ministry of Education and the Central Epidemic Command Center.
