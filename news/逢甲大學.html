Feng Chia University English summer camp updates

逢甲大學來函通知原訂於110年6月30日至110年7月7日舉辦「2021 CDIO SUMMER CAMP in FCU-The Undying Life of Plastics-Trace the Microplastics!」英語夏令營隊，將調整於110年7月5日至110年7月9日以線上方式進行。
Feng Chia University informed that the English summer camp was originally scheduled to be held from June 30, 110 to July 7, 110, "2021 CDIO SUMMER CAMP in FCU-The Undying Life of Plastics-Trace the Microplastics!" It will be conducted online from July 5th to July 9th, 110.
